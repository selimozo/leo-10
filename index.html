In the 2004/05 season, Lionel Messi made his official debut for FC Barcelona. At 17 years old, he played his first La Liga match on October 16, 2004, against Espanyol. Later in the season, on May 1, 2005, Messi scored his first senior goal for the club against Albacete, becoming the youngest scorer in Barcelona's history at the time. This season marked the start of Messi's professional career.

In the 2005/06 season, Lionel Messi became a regular part of FC Barcelona's first team. He played in more matches, including key games in La Liga and the UEFA Champions League. One notable performance was against Chelsea in the Champions League, where Messi's dribbling and pace caused problems for the English side, though he was unfortunately injured in the second leg. He also scored his first Champions League goal against Panathinaikos. Despite his season being cut short by injury, Messi played a role in Barcelona winning both La Liga and the Champions League.
